## Architecture notes

The pipeline uses lexical plus semantic retrieval before reranking. Every final answer includes citations, and each citation maps back to an indexed source chunk.

## Quality strategy

- Retrieval recall benchmarks
- LLM-as-judge plus human review sampling
- Weekly drift checks for stale knowledge
